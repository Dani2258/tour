    7,788 meters high.

  3. Passu Cones

    4. Between the Gojal valley of upper Hunza and the nine-colored valleys, Gulmit provides a significant tourist spot

  4. Rush Lake

    5. Another amazing in the Nagar Valley of Hunza is Rush Lake. Moreover, Nagar is one of the most attractive valleys.
    Above all, mesmerizing colors of Nagar valley will never be found; nothing is more beautiful than simplicity.
    Rush Lake in Nagar valley is an alpine lake with almost a surface elevation of 4,694 meters. The massive mountains
    surround the lake, and it is considered to be the highest lake in Pakistan.
